ChordKey

Input simply Chord
as Third-party Keyboard App.

What can do?

  • This App is able to enter the Chord of different suffix to easy Flick input.
  • It can select the key Chord to be placed in the Keyboard. (ex "C" "F" "Bb" "degree"...)
  • It can choose the mode of Chord to be placed in the Keyboard. (ex "diatonic" "minor" "7th"...)
  • Tension Chord may be entered by selecting from top of the scroll area.
